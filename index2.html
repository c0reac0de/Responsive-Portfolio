About Me

Welcome! I am an early childhood educator, beekeeper and yoga instructor. I am in the process of integrating my passions into a cohesive curriculm around local ecology, sustainability and lots of fun! Discover how cultures have been cherishing and celebrating the bee for thousands of years! Observe the brilliance of the honeybee and appreciate the power of simple everyday choices that can help all pollinators in our neighbourhoods, now and in the future.


I am interested in learning about the underlying principles of programming, web development and infrastructure. Working within a Montessori environment showed me the importance of organization in a learning environment, and it is amazing to notice similarities between programming strutcures, yoga asana sequencing, and the precision within a healthy colony of bees.
